Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Install guide marksmanmate.com

MarksmanMate (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the MarksmanMate mobile application and website at marksmanmate.com. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and your choices.

Information we collect

Account information

  • Name, email address, and profile details when you register or sign in
  • Google account identifier if you use Sign in with Google
  • Subscription or membership status where applicable

Shooting and activity data

  • Shoot sessions, scores, notes, and related metadata you enter
  • Firearms and ammunition records in your locker
  • Club, group, event, and messaging activity on the platform

Device and app data

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • Authentication tokens stored securely on your device
  • Local database cache for offline use, synced to our servers when online

Data from phone permissions (with your consent)

  • Camera and photos — images you choose to attach to sessions or analyse targets
  • Location — approximate location when you tag a session or event (only when you use location features)
  • Microphone — audio processed locally for the shot timer feature
  • Notifications — to deliver messages and alerts you opt into
  • Bluetooth — where supported for compatible accessories

How we use your information

  • Provide and improve the MarksmanMate service
  • Sync your data between devices and the marksmanmate.com platform
  • Enable social features such as clubs, groups, friends, and events
  • Send service-related notifications you have enabled
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data.

Where data is stored

Data is stored on secure servers operated for marksmanmate.com. Some data is cached locally on your device so the app works offline and syncs when connectivity returns.

Third-party services

  • Google Sign-In — authentication subject to Google’s Privacy Policy
  • Hosting and infrastructure — processors who help us run the service under contract

Data retention

We retain your account and activity data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Some records may be kept where required by law.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (including the UK and EEA under UK GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. Contact us to exercise these rights.

Children

MarksmanMate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact
